Price of essentials: Commerce Ministry to introduce app for real-time monitoring

The Commerce Ministry of the Government of Bangladesh has recently decided to launch a real-time app for monitoring the import, production, and marketing of essential commodities to keep their prices stable.

AHM Shafiquzzaman, Director General of Directorate of National Consumers' Right Protection (DNCRP) and additional secretary of the Commerce Ministry recently announced the decision at a seminar on monitoring market and prices during Ramadan at Chattogram.
